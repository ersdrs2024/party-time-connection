    Frequently Asked Questions

    What types of events do you cater to?

    We cater to a wide range of events including birthday parties, weddings, corporate gatherings, and more.

    What items do you offer for rent?

    We offer furniture, décor, audiovisual equipment, photo booths, tents, tables, chairs, and more.

    How do I place an order for rental items?

    You can place an order through our website or contact us directly.

    Are your rental items well-maintained and clean?

    We take pride in our attention to detail, ensuring all items are meticulously maintained and clean.

    What are the rental hours?

    Deliveries are between 8 AM and 6 PM. Special requests for outside this window will be considered but not guaranteed.

    How long is the rental period?

    The standard rental duration is up to 6 hours unless otherwise specified.

    When will you set up my rental?

    Setup typically begins an hour prior to the rental start time. We will confirm delivery and setup time at least a day in advance.

    Do you deliver to my area?

    Call to confirm delivery availability in your area and for pricing details.

    What types of payments are accepted?

    We accept cash, business checks, money orders, MasterCard, Visa, American Express, and Discover.

    Is a deposit required?

    Yes, a $50 deposit is required. Orders over $500 require a 50% deposit. The deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to future rentals if cancellation is 10 days in advance.

    How far in advance should I place my order?

    It is recommended to place your order as early as possible. We accommodate last-minute requests if possible.

    Can I get a copy of your insurance?

    Yes, we can provide a copy of our insurance and an endorsement if required for a county, school, or club. Additional charges may apply.

    What happens if there is bad weather?

    Contact us to discuss options if inclement weather affects your event.

    Does the rental include delivery?

    Delivery is included for the first five miles from zip code 33611. Additional miles are charged at $2 per mile.

    You can reach us by phone at (813) 270-6690 or email at [email protected].
